# Problem Statement — Music Discovery Graduation Project

**Related:** [architecture.md](./architecture.md) · [ROADMAP.md](./ROADMAP.md)

---

## Context

Choose **one** product: **Spotify** or **Gaana**.

You are a **Product Manager on the Growth Team**. The company has millions of users and a sophisticated recommendation system—but a large share of listening still comes from **repeat playlists**, **familiar artists**, and **previously discovered tracks**.

**Strategic goal:** Increase **meaningful music discovery** and reduce **repetitive listening**.

---

## Part 1 — Build an AI-Powered Review Discovery Engine

Before proposing any solution, build an AI system that analyzes user feedback at scale.

**Allowed stacks:** Claude, GPTs, agents, workflows, RAG, n8n, Zapier, Perplexity, or any AI-native stack.

**Analyze:**

- App Store reviews  
- Play Store reviews  
- Reddit discussions  
- Community forums  
- Social media conversations  

**Answer:**

- Why do users struggle to discover new music?  
- What frustrates them about recommendations?  
- What listening behaviors are they trying to achieve?  
- What causes repeated listening to the same content?  
- Which segments face different discovery challenges?  
- What unmet needs appear consistently?  

**Deliverable:** Public workflow link + **1 slide** in final deck explaining how it works.

---

## Part 2 — Validate Through User Research

AI insights are a starting point only. Conduct **5–6 user interviews** with respondents from your **chosen segment**.

**Deliverable:** Interview synthesis linked or summarized in deck (slide 5).

---

## Part 3 — Define the Problem

Based on research, articulate:

- **Root cause** of the problem  
- **Target user segment**  
- **Why solving it makes business sense**  

**Deliverable:** Problem statement artifact → feeds MVP scope.

---

## Part 4 — Build an AI-Native MVP

Design and build a **functional MVP** (feature prototype or agent). **Deploy to production.**

Explain:

- Why traditional recommendation systems are insufficient  
- What AI unlocks that was previously difficult  
- How AI changes the user experience  

**Deliverable:** Production URL + deck slide with screenshot.
