# Part 2 — User interview guide (30 min)

**Segment:** Indian urban Spotify users, 22–35, 5+ hrs/week, mostly saved playlists, rarely use Discover Weekly.

**Prerequisite:** Part 1 themes ready — show top 3 cards in second half.

---

## Block 1 — Warm-up (3 min)

- Walk me through what you listened to yesterday on Spotify.
- Where were you (commute, work, home)?

## Block 2 — Current behavior (8 min)

- When do you replay the same playlists vs try something new?
- What triggers going back to familiar music?
- Roughly what % of listening is Liked Songs / saved playlists?

## Block 3 — Recommendation products (8 min)

- Do you use Discover Weekly, Daily Mix, Radio, Blend? How often?
- What works? What feels off or repetitive?
- Last time you found a **new** artist you loved — how did that happen?

## Block 4 — Validate Part 1 themes (8 min)

Show theme cards from `artifacts/theme_map.json` top themes:

1. **Hard to find new music** — does this match your experience?
2. **Free tier / ads limit exploration** — if applicable
3. **Same content on loop** — comfort listening after work?

Probe: *What would disprove this for you?*

## Block 5 — Ideal outcome (3 min)

- What would “meaningful discovery” look like for you in one sentence?
- Would you want an coach/explanation before a discovery session, or just new tracks?
