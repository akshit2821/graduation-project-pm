## Surprise insight (not dominant in Part 1)

**Decision fatigue at transition moments** (6pm commute home) drives repeat listening more than “bad algorithms” alone. Users want a **short explanation + 3 options**, not another playlist feed (P01, P03, P05).

This refines Part 3 problem: *looping is a moment-of-day habit, not only a recommendation quality issue.*
